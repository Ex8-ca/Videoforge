📝 LTX-2 Prompting Guide

🎬 Key Elements (Write 4-8 sentences)

  • • Establish the shot - Use cinematography terms (close-up, wide shot, etc.)
  • • Set the scene - Lighting, colors, textures, atmosphere
  • • Describe the action - Natural sequence from start to end
  • • Define characters - Age, hair, clothing, emotions through physical cues
  • • Camera movement - Pan, track, dolly, zoom, handheld
  • • Audio/dialogue - Use quotes for speech, describe ambient sounds

✨ What Works Best

  • • Single flowing paragraph (present tense)
  • • Cinematic compositions with thoughtful lighting
  • • Emotive human moments, subtle gestures
  • • Weather effects: fog, mist, golden hour, rain
  • • Match detail to shot scale (closeups need more detail)

⚠️ What to Avoid

  • • Multiple disconnected scenes
  • • Overly complex camera movements
  • • Too many characters interacting
  • • Rapid scene changes
  • • Vague descriptions without visual details

📋 Example Prompt

"INT. OVEN – DAY. Static camera from inside the oven, looking outward through the slightly fogged glass door. Warm golden light glows around freshly baked cookies. The baker's face fills the frame, eyes wide with focus, his breath fogging the glass as he leans in. Baker (whispering dramatically): 'Today… I achieve perfection.' He leans even closer, nose nearly touching the glass. 'Golden edges. Soft center. The gods themselves will smell these cookies and weep.' Steam drifts upward in slow motion."

🎥 Helpful Camera Terms

Movement: tracks, pans, circles, tilts, pushes in, pulls back, dolly, crane
Shots: close-up, medium shot, wide shot, establishing shot, over-the-shoulder
Style: handheld, static frame, shallow depth of field, film grain
Effects: slow motion, motion blur, fade-in, fade-out, time-lapse
